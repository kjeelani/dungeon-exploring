Dungeon Exploring

In this game, you are stuck in a dungeon and you need to get out as quickly as possible. Everytime you move forward, there will be three tunnels that take you in different directions. Some tunnels can lead to monsters or traps that can kill you. However, you have your trusty DungeonMap3000 that can intelligently calculate the shortest path to escape.
